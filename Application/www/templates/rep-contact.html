Tips
Give your name, city, and zip code, and say "I don't need a response". This way they can tally you down without having to take time to issue a response.
State the issue, state your position. For example- "I am opposed to a ban on Muslims entering the US". That's it. Staffers will write down a tally of who is in favor, so it doesn't matter WHY you hold that opinion.
Be nice! The people answering the phones have some of the hardest jobs in Washington, and have some of the lowest pay as well. Be sure to thank them for their work.
